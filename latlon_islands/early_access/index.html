What are you getting?

In the zip you'll find 2 types of files: the shapefiles, useful for any geo/mapping project, and a vectorial, cleaned up version of them, something you can edit in Illustrator, Sketch, etc.

You will find the shapefiles for all 6 islands and the Mars notebook. The elevation lines are raw data files generated by us from high resolution GeoTIFF imagery. The interval between each elevation line is indicated in the filename.

These shapefiles can be opened by most GIS programs (we strongly recommend the Open Source QGIS) allowing you to further explore the islands. We also included each islands' coastline, with the data obtained from Open Street Maps database.

Use the vectorial designs in your own design projects.

If you aren't interested in getting into GIS, we have also included a PDF version of each islands' topology. These can be opened by most vector editing programs. They are almost the exact files we used for the notebook covers. They have been meticulously cleaned, simplified and smoothed so they could be letterpressed and would look their best on the covers of our notebooks.
